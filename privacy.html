Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Elevare processes and protects data when using the application.

1. General Information

This app ("Elevare") processes personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Collected

Elevare uses Firebase services provided by Google for anonymous authentication, subscription verification, and backend functionality.

When using the app, a unique anonymous user ID (UID) is generated and stored. The app does not require users to provide personal information such as their name or email address.

3. Legal Basis

Data is processed based on Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests in providing and maintaining the app).

4. Subscription and Payment Data

If a user purchases a subscription via Google Play, the following data may be processed:

  • Product ID
  • Subscription status
  • Purchase token
  • Order ID
  • Expiry time

This data is used solely for subscription verification and premium entitlement management.

5. Subscriptions

Elevare offers optional premium subscriptions (monthly and annual). Subscriptions are billed via Google Play and renew automatically unless canceled. Prices may vary depending on region and applicable taxes.

Users can manage or cancel their subscription at any time via their Google Play account settings.

If a subscription is not canceled before the end of the billing period, it will automatically renew.

6. Data Storage

Most user data is stored locally on the user's device using a local database. This includes tasks, missions, milestones, task completion history, display name, reminder settings, theme preferences, app settings, and locally cached subscription status information.

Certain data required for subscription verification and premium entitlement management is stored in Firebase Firestore.

7. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed exclusively for:

  • Providing app functionality
  • Managing subscriptions
  • Ensuring correct premium access

8. Third-Party Services

This app uses services provided by Google:

  • Firebase (authentication and database)
  • Google Play (in-app purchases)

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Data may be transferred to countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA), including the United States. Google uses standard contractual clauses and additional safeguards to protect personal data transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

10. Data Retention

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality. Locally stored data can be deleted at any time by using the in-app reset feature, clearing the app data via device settings, or uninstalling the app.

Cloud data associated with the current User ID can be deleted directly within the app through the Account page. Users should perform this action before uninstalling the app, as anonymous accounts may no longer be recoverable afterwards.

11. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their data
  • Request deletion of their data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Receive a copy of their data
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
