5. Subscription and Payment Data

If a user purchases a subscription via Google Play, the following data may be processed:

  • Product ID
  • Subscription status
  • Purchase token
  • Order ID
  • Expiry time

This data is used solely to verify subscription status and grant premium access.

Subscriptions

Elevare offers optional premium subscriptions (monthly and yearly). Subscriptions are billed via Google Play and renew automatically unless canceled.

Users can manage or cancel their subscription at any time via their Google Play account settings.

If a subscription is not canceled before the end of the billing period, it will automatically renew.

6. Data Storage

Most user data (such as tasks, missions, analytics, display name, and app preferences) is stored locally on the user's device.

Certain data required for subscription verification is stored in Firebase Firestore.
